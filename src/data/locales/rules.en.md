# Kingdom Legacy — Rules

## Goal

Develop your kingdom by upgrading your cards over multiple rounds. The game ends after the round containing card **#70**. Your final score is the total **Glory** ({{glory}}) across all your cards.

---

## Setup

Your starting kingdom is cards **1–10**, shuffled into a face-up draw deck. The remaining cards stay in the discovery pile — you will gradually unlock them round by round.

---

## Phases

### New Game

Click **New game** to shuffle your starting deck and enter the game.

### Between Rounds

At the start of each new round (from round 2 onwards), **2 new cards** are added to your deck from the discovery pile. If the next card is a **parchment card**, it immediately applies its effect and is destroyed.

The **Round Preview** screen shows which cards were just added before the first turn begins — click **▶ Start round** to continue.

### Playing

Turns run automatically one after another as long as cards remain in the draw pile. When the draw pile empties, the round ends and the next round begins.

---

## Turn Structure

Each turn starts by automatically drawing **4 cards** onto the board. If fewer than 4 cards remain, all are drawn.

You then interact with the cards freely, in any order, until you choose to end your turn:

| Action          | How                                                                                         |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Produce**     | Click a card's production button to gain its resources — the card is discarded.             |
| **Use effect**  | Click a card's action button. A cost may need to be paid first.                             |
| **Upgrade**     | Click an upgrade path on a card, pay the cost — the card upgrades and **the turn ends**.    |
| **›› Progress** | Draw **2 more cards** onto the board (can be done multiple times, as long as cards remain). |
| **End turn**    | End your turn voluntarily without upgrading.                                                |

At the end of each turn, **all resources are lost** and all remaining board cards are discarded.

---

## Resources

Resources are produced by cards and spent to pay action or upgrade costs. They are **lost at the end of the turn** — spend them in the same turn you gain them.

## Card Mechanics

### Production

Clicking a card's production button gains its resources and **discards the card**. A card can only produce once per turn.

### Actions & Effects

Cards may have one or more actions. Activating an action can:

- Gain resources or Glory
- **Discover** a new card from the discovery pile into your deck
- **Upgrade** a target card to a new state
- **Discard**, **destroy**, or **block** cards
- **Play** a card from the discard pile
- Add a **sticker** to a card

Costs can require spending **resources**, **discarding** cards, or **destroying** cards.

### Upgrades

Each card can have multiple upgrade paths. Upgrading replaces the card's current state with a new, more powerful version. **Upgrading always ends your turn.**

### Card States

Some cards have **multiple sides** (states). When such a card is discovered, you choose which state to activate.

### Tracks

Some cards have a **progress track** — a row of square buttons displayed on the card. Each button is a **step** that can be activated once by paying its cost (shown above the button).

Step content shows what you gain: a **Glory** value, a **resource**, a **card number** to discover, or an **↑** arrow for an upgrade.

- If the track is **in order**, only the first unvalidated step is clickable.
- If the track is **free**, any unvalidated step can be clicked independently.

Once clicked, a step is permanently **checked (✓)** and cannot be activated again. Validated steps count towards your Glory score at the end of the game.

### Stickers

Stickers are persistent modifiers applied to card instances. They can increase a card's production output or add Glory points.

### Permanents

Permanent cards stay on the board at all times — they are **never discarded** at the end of a turn. Their effects are always active.

---

## Pending Effects

Some cards trigger effects automatically when drawn or discovered. When **multiple effects** are pending at once, a panel appears letting you choose the **resolution order**. You may also **skip** optional effects.

---

## Undo

The **↩ button** (top-right) lets you undo the last action. Use it to correct mistakes before ending your turn.

---

## Scoring

At the end of the final round (after card #70), your score is the total **Glory** ({{glory}}) on all cards in your kingdom — draw pile, discard pile, and permanents included.
